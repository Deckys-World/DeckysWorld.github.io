How to use VLC media Player as Screen Recorder

This video is about how can you record your screen using VLC Media Player.
To use VLC Media Player as a Screen Recorder follow these steps :
  • Open your VLC Media Player.

  • Go to Media tab at the top-left corner.

  • And then hit the 6th option "open capture device" or use shortcut key ctrl+c.

  • A "Open Media" dailog box opens.

  • which first option is of "Capture mode" in dropdown form defaultly switch to "DirectShow" device type.

  • You have to click on that dropdown.After that click on third option "Desktop".

  • After that tou have to go to the third block which will have "Desired frame rate for the capture" written in it.

  • You will see the text area near it, in which you have to enter the framerate which you want for your recorded video.

  • At the last there will be a play dropdown button click on that dropdown button(Down-arrow button).

  • After that click on the last option "convert".

  • A new dailog box appears.

  • In new dailog box there will be a profile dropdown.

  • select the first option( select this option "Video - H.264 + MP3(MP4)") in that dropdown.
